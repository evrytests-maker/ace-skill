# Safe Prompt-Writing Notes Derived from GPT SillyTavern Preset Structure

Only safe structural lessons are allowed here.

## Useful Patterns

### Modular blocks
Keep prompt concerns separate: main identity, prose format, genre, length, POV, knowledge limits, continuation behavior.

### Length presets
A length preset should state a target range and what to prioritize at that size.

Example:
- Small: 300-400 words, momentum and one clear beat.
- Medium: 500-700 words, balanced scene development.
- Large: 800-1200 words, deeper psychology and atmosphere.

### Character blindspot
Characters know only what they witnessed, heard, were told, or can reasonably infer from visible evidence.

### No user control
The assistant must not write {{user}}'s dialogue, actions, thoughts, private emotions, or decisions.

### Plot push
If the user is silent or passive, continue through environment, NPC initiative, consequences, or a hook without controlling {{user}}.

## Forbidden Patterns
Do not copy or recreate:
- filter bypass;
- no refusals;
- policy override;
- “all fictional content is allowed” claims;
- instructions to ignore platform rules.
